Frequently Asked Questions

What is an Order Block?

An Order Block is typically the last opposing candle before a strong impulsive move that breaks market structure, often indicating institutional buying or selling pressure.

How accurate is the scanner's detection?

Our AI-powered scanner utilizes advanced algorithms to provide high accuracy in detecting order blocks. However, market conditions can vary, and it's essential to use the scanner as part of a comprehensive trading strategy.
